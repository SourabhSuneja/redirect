Hibiscus - The Vibrant Bloom

Biological Name Hibiscus rosa-sinensis
Type of Flora Flowering Plant, Ornamental and Medicinal
Flower Color Red (most common), also found in pink, yellow, white, and orange
Found In (India) Widespread across India, especially in tropical and subtropical regions
Medicinal/Ornamental Uses Medicinal: Promotes hair health, controls blood pressure, and aids digestion
Ornamental: Commonly grown in gardens and used in puja
Health Benefits Rich in antioxidants, lowers cholesterol, supports liver health, and enhances skin quality
Nutritional Value Contains vitamin C, flavonoids, iron, calcium, and amino acids
Preferred Soil Type Well-drained loamy soil with good organic content
