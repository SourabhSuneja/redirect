Giloy - The Root of Immortality

Biological Name Tinospora cordifolia
Type of Flora Climbing Shrub, Medicinal Plant
Flower Color Yellowish-green
Found In (India) Throughout tropical India, especially in forests and gardens
Medicinal/Ornamental Uses Medicinal: Immunity booster, fever reducer, anti-inflammatory
Ornamental: Sometimes grown on fences or trellises
Health Benefits Boosts immunity, purifies blood, reduces stress and anxiety, fights respiratory infections
Nutritional Value Rich in alkaloids, glycosides, steroids, and polysaccharides with antioxidant and antimicrobial properties
Preferred Soil Type Well-drained soil with moderate moisture; can grow in poor soil conditions
