Amla - The Vitamin C Powerhouse

Biological Name Phyllanthus emblica
Type of Flora Fruit-bearing Tree, Medicinal Plant
Fruit Color Greenish-yellow
Found In (India) Uttar Pradesh, Madhya Pradesh, Tamil Nadu, and widely across India
Medicinal/Ornamental Uses Medicinal: Boosts immunity, improves eyesight, aids digestion
Ornamental: Grown in home gardens for health benefits
Health Benefits Extremely rich in Vitamin C, promotes hair growth, detoxifies body, controls blood sugar
Nutritional Value Vitamin C, iron, calcium, fiber, antioxidants, and polyphenols
Preferred Soil Type Well-drained loamy to light clay soils
