Class 8 - Data Science Lesson Plan

Book: Orange Touchpad Data Science

Chapter Learning Objectives Teaching Methods Activities & Practical Sessions Expected Outcomes Estimated Classes
Ch-1: Introduction to Data Understanding data types, sources, and importance of data. Interactive lecture, discussion, real-world examples. Activity: Identify types of data in daily life.
Practical: Simple data collection task.		 Students will be able to classify and collect basic data. 3 Classes
Ch-2: Introduction to Data Science Understanding the fundamentals of data science and its applications. Presentation, videos, case studies. Activity: Research and present real-world data science applications. Students will grasp the significance of data science in various industries. 4 Classes
Ch-3: Data Visualization Understanding different types of data visualization techniques. Hands-on session, practical exercises. Practical: Creating charts using Google Sheets/MS Excel. Students will be able to create and interpret different types of graphs. 4 Classes
Ch-4: Data Science and Artificial Intelligence Exploring the role of AI in data science. Interactive discussion, AI demonstration videos. Activity: AI vs. Human decision-making challenge. Students will understand how AI is applied in data science. 5 Classes
