ScanNBill App
Devanshi & Nidhi (Class 9) | Android

Hello everyone!

We are Devanshi and Nidhi from Class 9.

Our project is a Mini Shop Model with a Barcode-Based Android App called ScanNBill.

This app can generate barcodes for products, which we can print and stick on the items.

Then, by scanning the barcode using a phone, the product is added to the cart automatically.

The app calculates the total bill and also records all the sales.

We developed this using basic app development tools and simple logic for barcode scanning and billing.

This shows that we don’t need expensive machines—just a mobile phone can work as a smart billing system.

We took help from our computer teacher Sourabh Sir and used AI tools like Gemini and Claude to give it a final touch.
