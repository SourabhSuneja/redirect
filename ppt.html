What is Alzheimer's Disease?

ü§î

Alzheimer's is a brain disease that mainly affects older people, usually those over 65 years old.

Think of your brain like a library...

Alzheimer's makes it hard for people to find and remember the "books" (memories) in their brain library.

It's the most common cause of memory problems in elderly people.
