Class 6 - Computer Science

Topic Coverage Plan (April 3 - April 5, 2025)

Date Topic Learning Objectives Resources Used Pedagogy Learning Outcomes Art Integrated Activity Assignment
April 3, 2025 First & Second Generation Languages Understand early programming languages and their limitations. Textbook, PPT, YouTube Video Lecture + Discussion Students will differentiate between first & second-gen languages. Draw a timeline showing evolution of programming languages. Write 5 differences between first & second-gen languages.
April 4, 2025 Third & Fourth Generation Languages Understand advancements in programming languages. Textbook, Online Articles Interactive Presentation + Q&A Students will compare third & fourth-gen languages. Design a poster showcasing examples of modern languages. List 3 modern programming languages and their uses.
April 5, 2025 Translators (Assemblers, Interpreters & Compilers) Understand different types of language translators. Textbook, YouTube Video, Live Demo Class Discussion + Practical Demo Students will explain the role of assemblers, interpreters, and compilers. Create a flowchart showing how compilers & interpreters work. Complete textbook exercises related to language translators.
